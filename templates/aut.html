HOW TO PLAY SLOTS

SLOTS RULES

Slots really cannot be more easier. You simply select bet amount, and press play.

Slot machine will spin and show 3 numbers next to each other.

You win when those 3 numbers are the same.


BET TYPES

At top left you can see how much you win with specific trio of numbers

- Red pays 1 to 1    ||    Win chance: 48.60%

- Black pays 1 to 1    ||    Win chance: 48.60%

- Even pays 1 to 1    ||    Win chance: 48.60%

- Odd pays 1 to 1    ||    Win chance: 48.60%

- Straight up on any single number pays 35 to 1    ||    Win chance: 2.70%
