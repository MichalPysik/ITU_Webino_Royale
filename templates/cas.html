HOW TO PLAY AT CASINO ROYALE


USER MENU

At bottom left of your screen you can see your user menu.

This menu contains:

- Name of user, which is logged in
- Balance of user, which is logged in

Button "Change user" brings you back to loggin screen, where you can change users.

Button "Help" brings up popup, which shows you this help screen.


GAMES

At the bottom of the screen are three games to choose from

The game on the left is called Roulette and it is a classic European Roulette

The middle game is called Dice

The game on the right is called Slots
