HOW TO PLAY DICE

DICE RULES

You, as a player, play against a computer controlled enemy. Whoever throws the bigger sum of numbers wins.

You can choose the amount of dice with which both you and the enemy throws. All the values will be shown in the table on the left.

When a game is played, algorithm randomly generates numbers from 1 to 6 for each simulated throw.

You choose the amount you bet before you click the throw button. If you win, the bet amount is added to your balance, if you lose, it gets substracted, and if the game ends in a draw, your balance stays unchanged.

GAME CONTROLS

Enter the bet amount as a number into the BET text field.

Select the amount of dice being thrown by each player.

Press the Throw button to start the game.
