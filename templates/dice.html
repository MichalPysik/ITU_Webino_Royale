HOW TO PLAY DICE

DICE RULES

Roulette is a classic casino game consisting of a wheel partitioned into the numbers 0-36, and a table on which to place bets.

Numbers 1-36 on the wheel are coloured red or black, while the 0 is green.

When a game is played, algorithm selects one of the numbers 0-36, and this will be the winning number.

Bets are placed on table before the Bet button is clicked, and these bets can be placed on a variety of different outcomes.

GAME CONTROLS

Click on a specific number, or a betting choice in betting area to place a bet. You can place a bet on any of the active betting fields.

Select "2x" to double your current bet
